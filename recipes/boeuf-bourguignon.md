# Boeuf Bourguignon

## Ingredients

2kg beef cheeks

8 carrots

8 onions

1kg sweet silver skin onions

4 bay leaves

Thyme

6 packs mushrooms

1kg pancetta do not trim pepper crust (4 blocks from Aldens)

1L beef stock minimum

## Method

Dry brine the beef

Discard first veg

Reduce sauce

Carrots in skillet butter olive oil, char them

Onions ditto then braise in beer
