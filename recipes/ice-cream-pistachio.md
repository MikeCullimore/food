# Pistachio ice cream

Eddie loves this!

## Ingredients

- 300mL whole milk
- 230g granulated sugar
- 600mL double cream
- 300g pistachio cream (Aldi is fine)
- 180g dark chocolate (suggest 54%)

## Method

1. Freeze ice cream machine bowl overnight.
1. Whisk sugar into milk until dissolved.
1. Whisk in the cream.
1. Whisk in the pistachio cream. 
1. Chill the mixture for at least two hours, better still overnight. 
1. Pour the mixture into the machine, churn for 30 minutes. 
1. While it's churning, chop the chocolate fine. 
1. Pour the chocolate into the mix when there's five minutes to go.
1. Pour the ice cream into an airtight container, press parchment onto the surface then put in the freezer.
