# Potatoes dauphinois

## Ingredients

3kg potatoes e.g. Maris Pipers
1.2L double cream 
6 cloves garlic
Parmesan
Freshly ground black pepper
Sea salt

## Equipment

Rectangular baking dish
Mandolin (could cut by hand but slower and less consistent).
Balloon whisk

## Method

Peel the potatoes (store in water).
Grate the potatoes 3mm thick (mandolin: use the guard!).
Grate garlic into cream. 
Season the cream, heavy on the pepper.
Repeat until dish full:
    One layer of potatoes 
    Stir the cream
    Pour layer of cream
Layer of parmesan in the middle for seasoning. 
Should have light covering of cream at the end.
